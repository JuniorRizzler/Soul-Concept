How It Works

Cards now reappear a few minutes to a few hours after you rate them. Lower ratings bring cards back sooner, higher ratings push them further out, and the mode you choose changes how aggressive that spacing is.

This is good because spaced recall makes you revisit a card right before you are likely to forget it, which is usually stronger than rereading everything at once.
