Community Driven

Built by students, shaped by students

This project is community-driven. Every subject added, every feature built, every improvement made - it all happens because students like you share what they need. I hope to expand to more subjects and higher grades and turn this into a fully working PWA and mobile app.

Instead of scattered pages across OneNote and Canvas that you need to fill in, all the information is organized in one place, with clear sections, visual aids, an AI assistant, practice quizzes, flash cards, worksheets, and a study schedule planner tailored to the Appleby curriculum.

Forget uploading, retyping questions, and searching through past chats with GPT or Notebook LM. The capabilities of both are pre-made for Appleby students by an Appleby student.
